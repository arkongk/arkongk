## About

Undergraduate student in Computer Science and Medicinal Chemistry.

I work at the intersection of computation and molecular science, applying algorithms, scientific programming, and machine learning to problems in drug discovery. My focus is on building precise, reproducible, and technically rigorous systems that turn chemical data into usable models and research infrastructure.

Open source, scientific computing, Linux systems, and hardware-oriented experimentation are part of the same continuum: control, clarity, and reproducibility.

> *Code is method. Infrastructure is leverage. Molecules are just another system to understand.*
